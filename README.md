### 🔬 Technical Case Study: "808" Synthesis
The featured project in this portfolio, **"808"**, serves as a ground-truth evaluation for multimodal generative models.

**Key Technical Evaluations:**
* **Temporal Consistency:** Testing the model's ability to maintain character and environment stability across complex scene transitions.
* **Video Physics:** Assessing lighting refractions, fluid dynamics (Teal Liquid Glass), and kinetic realism in high-fidelity outputs.
* **Audio-Visual Alignment:** Synchronizing custom 808-heavy transients (Composed by **Price Royalty Publishing**) with generative motion to evaluate "rhythmic intelligence" in AI video models.
